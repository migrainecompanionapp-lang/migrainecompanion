Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about MIgraine Companion and Mi, your gentle migraine companion.

We've compiled answers to the most common questions about Migraine Companion. Can't find what you're looking for?

hello@mi-companion.com
What makes Migraine Companion different from other trackers?

We don't just count migraines — we use narrative therapy. Mi is your migraine represented as a companion. Instead of fighting, you learn to understand and care. This is a fundamentally different approach to living with chronic pain.

What is "companionization"?

It's a narrative therapy technique: you externalize the problem. Mi is your migraine separate from you. You're not "a migraineur" — you're a person who lives with Mi. This reduces self-blame and increases your sense of control over the situation.

Do I need a therapist to use the app?

No. Narrative therapy is built into the app's design. Mi itself is a therapeutic tool. You get the benefits of a therapeutic approach simply by using the app every day.

Is the app free?

Yes. All essential features for managing migraine are free forever. This is not a trial or limited version. You can effectively track migraines, find triggers, and care for yourself — completely free.

What's included in the free version?

Everything you need to live with migraine:

  • Complete migraine journal and trigger tracking
  • Tracking 43 factors — food, sleep, weather, stress, hormones
  • Mi's mood stones — visualization of your state
  • Medication tracking with WHO safety limits
  • Food trigger detection with smart parsing
  • Weather sensitivity tracking
  • Calendar view
  • Basic data export to PDF
What is Premium subscription?

Premium is for those who want AI assistance and deep analytics. Includes four additional features:

  • Mi's AI Forecast — personalized 24-hour migraine prediction based on machine learning
  • Therapeutic Games — Breathing Garden with 4-7-8 technique and Migraine Monster for narrative therapy practice
  • Advanced Reports — detailed reports with graphs, patterns, and insights
  • Clinical Reports — MIDAS assessment and CGRP tracking specifically for your neurologist
Why are some features paid?

AI predictions require server resources and ongoing development. Therapeutic games and advanced analytics are for power users who want the maximum from the app.

But importantly: we don't hide core functionality behind a paywall. Migraine tracking, trigger detection, medication safety — all free. Unlike many competitors who limit even basic features with subscriptions.

Can I try Premium for free?

Yes. Premium is available with a free trial period — you can test all features risk-free and cancel anytime.

Is my data safe?

Absolutely. All data is stored locally on your device. We don't sell, share, or analyze your personal data. Your health information belongs only to you.

Can I sync between devices?

In development. Soon you'll be able to sync data via iCloud with full end-to-end encryption. Even we won't be able to read your data.

Can I export my data?

Yes. You can export a complete report in PDF format for your doctor or personal archive. Your data is always accessible and portable.

What happens to my data if I delete the app?

All data is deleted from the device. We don't store copies on servers. We recommend exporting your data before deletion.

Does the app work offline?

Yes. You don't need internet for core tracking. Internet is only required for:

  • Weather data
  • Mi's AI forecast
  • Device synchronization (when available)
How accurate is Mi's AI forecast?

Mi learns from your data over 2-4 weeks. Accuracy grows over time — usually reaches 70-85% after a month of regular use. The more data, the more accurate the forecast.

Can I add my own triggers?

Yes. Mi tracks 43 standard factors based on medical research, but you can add your unique triggers that are important specifically for you.

Does the app support Apple Health / Google Fit?

iOS — yes, full integration with Apple Health for syncing sleep, activity, and other health metrics.

Android — Google Fit integration will be added after the Android release.

How much time does daily tracking take?

1-2 minutes per day. Mi doesn't require much effort — quick logging of food, sleep, mood. The app is designed not to be a burden in your life.

What if I forget to log data?

It's okay. Mi understands — life with migraine is unpredictable. Just continue when you can. Even incomplete data helps find patterns.

Is the app based on real science?

Yes. Narrative therapy is a scientifically validated approach developed by Michael White and David Epston. Used for over 40 years to treat chronic pain, depression, and anxiety disorders.

Did you consult with neurologists?

The app is built on:

  • 30 years of personal experience living with migraine
  • WHO guidelines on medication safety
  • Medical research on migraine triggers
  • Narrative therapy principles from the founders of the approach
Can the app replace a doctor?

No. Mi is a tool for self-help and self-understanding. It helps you understand your personal patterns and manage your condition, but doesn't replace professional medical consultation. Always discuss serious changes with your neurologist.

What age is the app suitable for?

The app is suitable for teenagers 13+ and adults. For younger children, we recommend using it together with parents.

Is the app available in other languages?

Currently only English is available. In development:

  • Russian
  • Ukrainian
  • Spanish

Other languages will be added based on user requests.

Do I need to pay for updates?

No. All app updates are completely free. New features are added regularly at no additional cost.

Who created Migraine Companion?

Rustam Iuldashov — a solo developer from Ukraine with 30 years of experience living with migraine. Created the app he wished existed — not a cold medical tracker, but an understanding companion.

Why did you create this app?

I was tired of clinical trackers that treat migraine as statistics. I needed a friend who understands — not a judge who counts attacks. That's how Mi was born.

Was the app developed in Ukraine during the war?

Yes. The app was created during wartime, often with power outages of 10-16 hours a day. But it was in the darkness that I realized — many people need light that brings understanding and acceptance of their condition.

Which devices are supported?
  • iOS 15+ — iPhone and iPad
  • Android — available on Google Play
How much storage does the app take?

About 120-130 MB — very little for a modern app. Mi includes an AI model, therapeutic games, graphs, and visualizations, but we've optimized everything for minimal size. For comparison: Instagram takes ~250 MB.

Does the app drain battery quickly?

No. Mi is optimized for minimal battery usage. The app runs in the background only when necessary and doesn't affect device battery life.

What if the app isn't working correctly?

Write to us at our support email or through the form in the app. We respond to all requests and fix issues as a priority.
